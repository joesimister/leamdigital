Our Story

Started with one bag of beans and a dream.

We opened The Warwick Grind because Warwick deserved better coffee.

Founder Sarah had spent years working in specialty cafés in London and Birmingham before returning home to Warwickshire. She couldn't find a single place locally that took coffee as seriously as she did — so she fixed that.

Every coffee is made with beans roasted by our friends at Outpost Roasters in Leamington. Every plate of food starts with local producers and changes with the seasons.
